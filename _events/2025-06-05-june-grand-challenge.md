This month's event was the last of the academic year, used to get to know the
MOSAIC community better, give an overview of the themes discussed in the past, and
discuss the best way to go forward in 2025–2026. Members were invited to help
decide the crucial themes to address next year and which research teams to invite
to host a session — with a particular aim of having more sessions chaired by
external members outside the core MOSAIC team.
