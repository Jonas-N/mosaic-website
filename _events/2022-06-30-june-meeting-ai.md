This month's meeting was a discussion and brainstorm about the promises and
pitfalls of artificial intelligence in the context of social interaction
research. The goal was to identify both the opportunities that modern AI tools
and models can offer and the problems associated with them.

After a short introduction and a presentation on a practical application of AI in
social interaction research, the group moved into breakout rooms, each dedicated
to a specific theme: AI as models of brain and behaviour, AI as experimental
tools, ethics, and data.
