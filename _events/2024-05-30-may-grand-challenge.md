We invited members to a discussion on alignment in multimodal interaction — a key
theme in human interaction research that has come up at several past MOSAIC
sessions. The event featured **Dr Marlou Rasenberg** (Meertens Institute,
Amsterdam), first author of a paper attempting to define the complex nature of
alignment along several dimensions in an integrative framework:

> Rasenberg, M., Özyürek, A., & Dingemanse, M. (2020). Alignment in Multimodal
> Interaction: An Integrative Framework. *Cognitive Science, 44*, e12911.

The paper served as a starting point before moving on to a broader discussion about
alignment in multimodal interactions.
