This session discussed a recently published paper, with both authors joining us:

> Trujillo, J. P., & Holler, J. (2024). Conversational facial signals combine into
> compositional meanings that change the interpretation of speaker intentions.
> *Scientific Reports, 14*(1), 2286.

The session began with a brief presentation of the paper by James, setting the
stage for a methods walkthrough and Q&A directly with the authors. It then moved
into a group discussion, inviting participants to share their perspectives on the
study of facial and eye signals — facial expressions, gaze patterns, and blinking
— and their impact on language interpretation and processing.
