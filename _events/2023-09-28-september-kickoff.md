To kick off a new academic year, this session took the form of an open general
discussion rather than a talk. It offered a platform for everyone to reintroduce
themselves, share perspectives, and discuss the focus and future of the MOSAIC
group — collaboratively identifying salient topics for upcoming sessions and
shaping a meaningful agenda for 2024.

In preparation, members were invited to think about: which topics they would like
to discuss in future; which speakers to invite; which papers could work for a
paper discussion with invited authors; which methods they would like to learn; and
any other uses for the meetings.
