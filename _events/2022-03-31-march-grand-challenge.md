This Grand Challenge took the form of a group discussion around a key paper:

> Hadley, L. V., Naylor, G., & Hamilton, A. F. de C. (2022). A review of theories
> and methods in the science of face-to-face social interaction. *Nature Reviews
> Psychology.*

Early-career researchers were invited to help lead the session — for example by
presenting a summary of one of the paper's sections — as part of MOSAIC's aim to
support ECRs in taking the lead on Grand Challenges.
