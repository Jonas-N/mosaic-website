# Calendar

Add the MOSAIC calendar to your own calendar app once, and every Grand Challenge
— including the Zoom joining link and a reminder — shows up automatically.

{% include subscribe-buttons.html %}

<div class="help-note" style="margin-top:16px">
  <strong>Which button?</strong>
  <ul style="margin:8px 0 0">
    <li><strong>Google Calendar</strong> (incl. Google Workspace): use <em>Add to Google Calendar</em>.</li>
    <li><strong>Apple Calendar or Outlook</strong>: use <em>Subscribe in Apple Calendar / Outlook</em> for a live, auto-updating subscription. If your app doesn’t open, use <em>Download .ics file</em> and add it manually.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
